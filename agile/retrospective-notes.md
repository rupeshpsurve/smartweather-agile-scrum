### Overall Project Retrospective

**Velocity Summary:**

| Sprint | Committed | Completed | Velocity |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sprint 1 | 37 | 37 | 37 |
| Sprint 2 | 26 | 21 | 21 |
| Sprint 3 | 26 | 26 | 26 |
| **Total** | **89** | **84** | **Avg: 28** |

**Top 3 lessons learned:**

1. **Vendor dependency management** — chase API documentation and credentials at least 1 week before sprint start. Resolving this in Sprint 1 prevented blockers in Sprints 2 and 3.

2. **Mid-sprint scope control** — without a formal change process, stakeholder requests can expand scope and compromise sprint delivery. The change request process introduced in Sprint 3 should be standard from Sprint 1 on future projects.

3. **Buffer for unknowns** — adding a 20% story point buffer for vendor-dependent stories gave the team room to absorb the unexpected API rate limiting issue without impacting delivery.
